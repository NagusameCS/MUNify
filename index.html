🌍 MUNify
Info & Permissions Form Designer

Run MUNify using your own Google Sheets.

No servers. No data on us. You sign in with Google, point to your Apps Script Web App and Sheet, and we do the rest right in your browser.

We only store configuration locally in your browser. You can clear it anytime in Settings.

🚀 Quick Setup

  1. Click Sign in with Google.
  2. Paste your Apps Script Web App URL and Sheet ID in Settings.
  3. Go to the Form Designer and create your form.
  4. Share form/#YOUR_HASH with delegates.
